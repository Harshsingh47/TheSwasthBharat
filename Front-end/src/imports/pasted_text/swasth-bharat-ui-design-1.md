Design a fully responsive, modern, multi-page healthcare platform UI called "The Swasth Bharat" — a human-centered digital ecosystem that connects patients with verified doctors, provides health awareness content, and supports social impact initiatives across India.

🎯 Design Vision:
Create a platform that feels:

* Trustworthy (like healthcare systems)
* Simple and usable (like Practo)
* Warm and human-centered (not overly clinical)
* Informative and community-driven

Avoid generic layouts. Use a mix of clean SaaS design + storytelling sections.

---

🎨 DESIGN STYLE:

* Colors:

  * Primary: Soft Blue (#3B82F6)
  * Secondary: Mint Green (#34D399)
  * Accent: Warm Beige (#F5F5DC)
  * CTA: Slight orange/coral
* Typography:

  * Headings: Poppins / Inter Bold
  * Body: Inter Regular
* UI:

  * Rounded cards (12–16px radius)
  * Soft shadows
  * Spacious layout
  * Section-based background variations (white + beige)

---

📱 RESPONSIVENESS:

* Mobile-first design
* Breakpoints:

  * Mobile (0–640px)
  * Tablet (641–1024px)
  * Desktop (1025px+)
* Stack layouts vertically on mobile
* Use hamburger menu for navigation
* Large touch-friendly buttons
